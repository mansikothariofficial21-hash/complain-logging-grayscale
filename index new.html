Further Grievance Support

If your complaint isn't resolved in time.

Each level opens once the expected resolution time has passed. You move one step at a time. All earlier interactions stay linked to your case.

1
Customer Care & BranchFirst point of contact
Raise and track complaints from the support workspace, or visit your home branch for everyday concerns.
Expected resolution3 to 5 working days
2
Grievance Redressal OfficerLevel 2, regional
If your complaint isn't resolved at Level 1 in time, the Grievance Officer reviews it with the full history attached.
Opens after5 working days
3
Principal Nodal OfficerLevel 3, apex bank
The bank's senior grievance authority. Handles complaints that remain unresolved at the regional level.
Opens after10 working days
4
RBI OmbudsmanExternal, regulatory
Independent regulatory redressal under the RBI Integrated Ombudsman Scheme. Free of cost to customers.
Eligible after30 days or final reply
Using further grievance support never affects your standing with the bank. Earlier interactions always stay linked to your case.
